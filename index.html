My Work

Posthaste Emporium

Posthaste Emporium Steam Page.
Posthaste Emporium is a chaotic fantasy store management game created in Unity as my capstone project at Niagara College. Posthaste Emporium was made by a team of 7 in 12 weeks.

Posthaste Emporium has amassed over 1,700 unique users since being published to steam in July of 2023.
